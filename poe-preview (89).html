Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about selling your home for cash

How does the cash offer process work? +

After you submit your information, we'll analyze your property and reach out within 24 hours with a fair cash offer. If you accept, we'll schedule a quick walkthrough, then proceed to closing on your timeline. The entire process can be completed in as little as 7 days.

Do I need to make any repairs before selling? +

No, we buy homes in any condition. You don't need to make any repairs, clean, or even remove unwanted items. We'll handle everything after the purchase.

Are there any fees or commissions? +

None at all. When we make an offer, that's the amount you'll receive at closing. We cover all closing costs, and there are no real estate agent commissions.

How quickly can I sell my house? +

We can close in as little as 7 days, or on your timeline. You choose the closing date that works best for your situation.

Is your cash offer guaranteed? +

Yes, our offers are guaranteed with no financing contingencies. When we make an offer, we have the funds ready to purchase your home.
